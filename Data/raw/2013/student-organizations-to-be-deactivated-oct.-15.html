October 11, 2012

Student organizations to be deactivated Oct. 15

WEST LAFAYETTE, Ind. — Purdue University's Student Activities and Organization Office announced the list of organizations and clubs whose records have become outdated.

Every semester - by Sept. 15 and Feb. 15 - organizations are required to submit an election report online at http://www.getinvolved.purdue.edu. In addition, within three weeks of an election for president, treasurer or adviser, clubs are required to report these changes into the same online system. Organizations that fail to update fall election reports will be deactivated on Monday (Oct. 15).
